SHIMA Edge Vented, Motorcycle Shoes for Men Summary



These motorcycle sneakers prioritize both protection and comfort for riders. Constructed with reinforced heel, dual ankle support, and a durable toe cap with a gear shift pad, these boots safeguard your feet while extending their longevity. Easy to slip on and off, they feature a Quick Fasten & Release ATOP function along with a rear pulling strap. Perforated leather strategically incorporated into the design ensures optimal ventilation during rides or long days, promoting breathability. These boots are crafted to excel on the bike while maintaining a sleek appearance that complements any vintage motorcycle jacket, available in classic black or brown
