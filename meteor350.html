Description

The Royal Enfield Meteor 350 is a modern cruiser motorcycle that seamlessly merges classic aesthetics with contemporary engineering. At its heart is a 349cc single-cylinder engine, which not only delivers a smooth and refined performance but also produces the signature Royal Enfield thumping exhaust note. With its relaxed riding posture, comfortable saddle, and user-friendly features, the Meteor 350 is designed for long, leisurely rides. It incorporates modern technology such as a responsive touchscreen navigation system, making it a versatile choice for riders. The bike's retro-inspired design, including its teardrop tank and chrome embellishments, adds a touch of nostalgia to its overall appeal. The Royal Enfield Meteor 350 offers a perfect blend of classic styling and modern conveniences, making it an attractive option for those seeking a cruiser with a dash of timeless charm.

The Meteor 350 returns a good 40 kmpl in the city but on the highway, this figure drops to around 350 kmpl. The Meteor 350 gets its power from a BS6-compliant 349 cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled, long-stroke motor. Linked to a new five-speed gearbox, the engine makes 20.2 bhp of power and 27 Nm of peak torque.The torque delivery is almost flat making it super easy to ride on the highway and city. Plus, the combination of light clutch and smooth gearshifts makes the riding experience more fun and comfortable.
