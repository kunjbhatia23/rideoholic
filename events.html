INDIA BIKE WEEK

India Bike Week 2023, one of the most significant motorcycling events in the country, is set to rev up the enthusiasm of bike enthusiasts and riders alike. Scheduled to take place in 2023, this mega two-wheeler extravaganza is a celebration of India's diverse motorcycle culture, attracting bikers from all corners of the nation. The event promises a thrilling amalgamation of live music, stunt shows, custom bike exhibitions, and an array of biking competitions. It provides a platform for bikers to showcase their passion and share their riding experiences, while also fostering a sense of community and camaraderie. SUZUKI MATSURI

Suzuki Matsuri 2023, also known as the Suzuki Festival, is a highly anticipated cultural and automotive event organized by Suzuki Motor Corporation. It is a grand celebration that combines the rich traditions of Japanese culture with the latest innovations in the world of automobiles. Scheduled for 2023, the festival offers a unique and immersive experience for attendees, featuring a showcase of Suzuki's cutting-edge vehicles, including motorcycles, cars, and even concept vehicles that provide a glimpse into the future of transportation. In addition to the automotive aspect, Suzuki Matsuri also highlights Japanese arts, crafts, and cuisine, giving visitors a taste of the country's heritage. The event typically includes live performances, traditional music, dance, and art exhibitions, creating a vibrant and diverse atmosphere. Suzuki Matsuri is an opportunity for enthusiasts and the general public to appreciate Suzuki's contributions to the automotive industry and experience the beauty of Japanese culture, all in one thrilling and culturally enriching festival. It's an occasion that blends modernity with tradition, making it a must-visit for automotive and cultural enthusiasts alike.

Date : 1st October

Destination : Delhi

Duration : 1 Day

Price : Rs. 499
