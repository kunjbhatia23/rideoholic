Description

The Hero Glamour Xtec is a commuter motorcycle that offers a harmonious blend of style, technology, and practicality. Powered by a refined 125cc engine, it delivers a balance of performance and fuel efficiency, making it a suitable choice for daily commuting and urban rides. The Glamour Xtec features a contemporary design with sharp lines and a sleek LED headlamp. Its key highlight is the inclusion of technological advancements, such as Bluetooth connectivity and turn-by-turn navigation through the Hero Connect app, offering convenience and connectivity on the go. With a comfortable and upright riding posture, combined with telescopic front forks and twin shock absorbers at the rear, the Glamour Xtec offers a smooth and comfortable ride. It's a well-rounded motorcycle, ideal for riders seeking a stylish and tech-savvy commuter with a touch of modernity in the 125cc segment.

Hero Glamour Xtec Summary



Hero Glamour Xtec is a mileage bike available in 2 variants and 4 colours. Glamour Xtec price starts at Rs. 1,01,403 in India with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,06,414. The Hero Glamour Xtec is powered by 124.7cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 10.72 bhp and a torque of 10.6 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Hero Glamour Xtec comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Glamour Xtec bike weighs 122 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 10 liters.

The Hero Glamour Xtech is a modern version of the standard Xtec. This model gets updated colours and some new cool features that have made the motorcycle more aspirational.

One of the major additions to this bike is the fully digital instrument cluster. Due to this, the rider will be able to access SMS and call alerts as there's Bluetooth integration. There's also a built-in USB phone charger that makes the bike more practical. The company also claims that the headlight is brighter. The new Glamour XTec also features a bank angle sensor that turns off the engine in an event of a fall. Cosmetic upgrades include a new matte black paint with silver accents, 3D branding, and silver rim tapes.

In terms of performance, the five-speed 124.7cc BS6 engine of Hero Glamour makes 10.7bhp of power and 10.6Nm of peak torque. The motorcycle comes up with an Idle Stop-Start system that is aimed to improve fuel efficiency. The motorcycle is built around a diamond frame, with telescopic forks at the front and hydraulic shock absorbers at the back. The braking power comes from 130mm drum brakes on both 18-inch alloy wheels while a 240mm disc brake is available as an option. The safety net includes CBS tech that Hero MotoCorp calls Integrated Braking System.

The Glamour competes with the Honda SP 125 and the Bajaj Pulsar 125. The bike is currently available in black, red and grey paint schemes.
