Hero

Hero MotoCorp Ltd, formerly known as Hero Honda, is the world's largest manufacturer of two-wheelers, headquartered in New Delhi, India. Established in 1984, it is a key player in the Indian and global motorcycle market.Hero MotoCorp is renowned for its wide range of motorcycles and scooters, designed to cater to a broad spectrum of customers. They are known for producing fuel-efficient, reliable, and affordable two-wheelers, which have made them extremely popular in India.

The company has a significant presence in the commuter bike segment and has produced many iconic motorcycles over the years. Their commitment to innovation and technology is evident in the introduction of products like the Hero Splendor, one of the best-selling motorcycles in the world.

Hero MotoCorp has also expanded its international footprint, exporting its vehicles to various countries. The brand's focus on sustainability is apparent through their green manufacturing practices and electric vehicle ventures, such as the Hero Electric brand. Hero MotoCorp's commitment to providing efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally friendly mobility solutions has solidified its position as a leader in the motorcycle industry, both in India and globally.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Hero MotoCorp and what does it manufacture?
Hero MotoCorp is the world's largest manufacturer of two-wheelers. It is an Indian multinational motorcycle and scooter manufacturer known for producing motorcycles and scooters that cater to a wide range of customers, from commuters to enthusiasts.
Where are Hero MotoCorp's manufacturing facilities located?
Hero MotoCorp has several manufacturing facilities in India. Their key plants are situated in places like Gurgaon, Dharuhera, Haridwar, Neemrana, and Halol. These plants are equipped to produce a variety of two-wheeler models.
What are some of the popular Hero MotoCorp motorcycle models?
Hero MotoCorp offers a diverse range of motorcycles, including popular models like the Hero Splendor, Hero Passion, Hero Xtreme, and Hero Glamour. These motorcycles are known for their reliability, fuel efficiency, and affordability.
Is Hero MotoCorp involved in any corporate social responsibility (CSR) initiatives?
Yes, Hero MotoCorp is actively engaged in CSR initiatives. They focus on areas like education, healthcare, and community development. Their 'Hero We Care' program embodies their commitment to social responsibility and community welfare. Hero MotoCorp has been recognized for its efforts in promoting education and healthcare in various communities.
