COFIT Motorcycle Gloves Breathable, Touchscreen Motorbike Gloves Summary



Versatile and adaptable, these COFIT gloves serve multiple outdoor activities, from motocross and road racing to cycling and climbing. They prioritize control and safety with wear-resistant silicone gel dots on the palm for a reliable grip on handlebars. Featuring touchscreen-compatible fingertips, thanks to conductive metal fibers on the index finger and thumb, these gloves enable easy device usage. Offering full hand protection, they boast superior hard PVC fist knuckle gear and palm pads. Crafted from breathable and durable polyester, these gloves ensure comfort throughout the year, suitable for temperatures ranging between 10°C to 38°C (50℉-100℉).
