JS FILMS
JS Films, also known as Jasminder Singh, is a prominent YouTuber in the bike enthusiast community. With a strong passion for motorcycles, JS Films has cultivated a dedicated following by producing captivating and informative content about various two-wheeled machines. His YouTube channel is a treasure trove of high-quality motorcycle reviews, thrilling rides, and insightful tips for both seasoned riders and newcomers to the world of motorcycling. JS Films' charismatic on-screen presence and in-depth knowledge of bikes have made him a respected figure in the industry, earning him a substantial subscriber base. Not only does he provide detailed reviews of the latest models and accessories, but he also takes viewers on exhilarating rides, sharing his adventures and experiences on the open road. JS Films' engaging videos and commitment to the biking community have solidified his status as a go-to source for all things related to motorcycles, making him a beloved figure among bike enthusiasts and a key influencer in the YouTube bike vlogging space.

RIDER GIRL VISHAKHA
RiderGirl Vishakha, also known as Vishakha Fulsunge, is a prominent figure in the Indian motorbike YouTube community. Her channel is renowned for breaking stereotypes in a male-dominated industry, as she passionately shares her biking adventures and experiences as a female rider. Vishakha's content ranges from in-depth motorcycle reviews to thrilling ride vlogs, and her engaging personality has earned her a significant following. She empowers and inspires women to pursue their love for biking and challenges societal norms, making her a trailblazer in the Indian bike YouTube scene.

XBHP
xBhp was founded as a single web page in 2001. Soon it grew to be a thriving online and offline community of India. It is still the largest and most popular motorcycling platform where people from all over the nation come and share their experiences and make like minded friends. Today besides an online forum and offline community it is also a portal, social media and a national print magazine by the same name.
