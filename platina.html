Description

The Bajaj Platina 110 is a practical and efficient commuter motorcycle designed to cater to the everyday riding needs of urban and suburban riders. Powered by a 115.45cc, DTS-i engine, it strikes a balance between power and fuel economy, ensuring a cost-effective and reliable ride. The bike's design is understated yet functional, featuring a comfortable long saddle, alloy wheels, and stylish graphics. The Platina 110 offers a comfortable upright riding posture, making it an ideal choice for daily commuting and long journeys. It comes with advanced features like LED headlamps, a digital console, and tubeless tires for added safety and convenience. With its excellent fuel efficiency, smooth performance, and comfortable ergonomics, the Bajaj Platina 110 is a practical and dependable option for riders seeking a no-nonsense, economical commuter motorcycle that gets the job done efficiently.

Yamaha MT 15 Summary



Bajaj Platina 110 is a mileage bike available in 3 variants and 9 colours. Platina 110 price starts at Rs. 84,889 in India with top variant price starting from Rs. 96,582. The Bajaj Platina 110 is powered by 115.45cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 8.48 bhp and a torque of 9.81 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Bajaj Platina 110 comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Platina 110 bike weighs 122 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 11 liters.

The Bajaj Platina 110 is the only motorcycle in its segment to feature the safety net of Anti-lock Braking System (ABS). The commuter motorcycle comes with a 240mm single disc with a single-channel ABS on the front wheel while the rear uses a 110mm drum. It is also available in a more affordable variant that gets a drum at both ends.

The changes, however, are limited to the addition of ABS to the motorcycle. The styling cues and the feature list remains unaltered, and the Bajaj Platina 110 ABS continues to use a halogen headlight with an LED DRL at the front, knuckle guards, quilted seats, a Nitrox spring-on-spring rear suspension, telescopic front forks, and alloy wheels with tubeless tyres.

Mechanical specifications, too, retain the 115 cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled motor that makes 8.4bhp at 7,000rpm and 9.81Nm of peak torque at 5,000rpm. The engine is linked to a five-speed gearbox.
